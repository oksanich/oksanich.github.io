About

Vltava Trading S.R.O. is a Prague, Czech Republic based company was established in 2009 to cater the boosting demand for spare parts supply for Czech and Ex-USSR made aircrafts, helicopters, ground units and installations that are being operated all over the world.

We render contemporaneous technical service, providing project spare parts supply and overhaul that is being made on the CIS repair plants. We have a big inventory of spare parts both new and after OH that can be dispatched to every shrouded airstrip in the world that is concomitant with our exceptional frugal and effective logistic service.

Our on-site engineers can assess and recapitulate any complicated operational issue and are able to execute repair, maintenance and technical follow-up under Customer's request both on the site and on the repair plant. Our incremental expertise in ground systems supply and overhaul can be an additional feature to our our high-scale service.

Our customers are private and Government Air Operators in Africa and Middle East whom we serve on the 24/7 basis with efficient response rate by excuting agile delivery within pressing time frames.
